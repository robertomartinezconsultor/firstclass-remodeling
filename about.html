Our Story

First Class Remodeling TX was founded by a husband‑and‑wife team born and raised in San Antonio. After years of working for large construction firms, they realised homeowners were often left feeling overwhelmed and unheard. They decided to create a company that values collaboration, transparency and craftsmanship. Today the team has grown to include experienced project managers, designers and artisans who share a passion for improving homes across neighborhoods from Alamo Heights to Helotes.

Our Values

  • Integrity: We say what we mean and deliver on our promises.
  • Quality: We use durable materials and proven techniques to build spaces that last.
  • Respect: We treat your home as if it were our own and keep disruption to a minimum.
  • Communication: We provide clear updates at every stage so you always know what to expect.
  • Community: We support local charities and participate in community events throughout San Antonio.

Community & Sustainability

We believe in giving back. Our team volunteers with Habitat for Humanity and sponsors youth sports leagues in Stone Oak. On every project we recycle old materials when possible and recommend water‑ and energy‑efficient fixtures that help reduce your environmental footprint.
